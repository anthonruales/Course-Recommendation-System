## Question Design Principles

1. **Clear Language**: Simple, direct Filipino-English wording
2. **Non-Overlapping Options**: Each option represents distinct traits
3. **Balanced Choices**: No obviously "better" answers
4. **Practical Scenarios**: Real situations students can relate to
5. **Progressive Depth**: General ‚Üí Specific ‚Üí Specialized questions
6. **No Right/Wrong**: All answers lead to valid course recommendations
